## 📖 Overview

This project presents an end-to-end analysis of customer shopping behavior using SQL, Python, and Power BI. The objective is to understand customer demographics, purchasing patterns, revenue trends, and customer satisfaction to generate meaningful business insights.

The project combines data cleaning, exploratory data analysis, SQL-based querying, and interactive dashboard development to provide a comprehensive view of customer behavior.

---

## ❓ Problem Statement

Retail businesses need to understand customer purchasing patterns and preferences to improve decision-making and maximize revenue.

This project aims to answer the following questions:

* Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
* Which age groups contribute the most to sales?
* How does customer loyalty affect spending?
* What are the trends in customer ratings?
* Which locations generate the highest revenue?
* How do customer demographics influence purchasing behavior?

## 💡 Key Business Insights

* Clothing emerged as the highest revenue-generating category.
* Customer spending varied across age groups.
* Subscription status influenced purchasing behavior.
* Customer ratings were concentrated between 3 and 4.
* Previous purchases indicated strong customer loyalty.
* Certain locations generated significantly higher revenue.
